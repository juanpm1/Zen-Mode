Welcome to Zen Mode!

This is a project made for practice web developing, just an space to enter to your zen mode and be a little more relaxed, enjoy and have fun!

Having a Zen attitude means finding mindful awareness of the present moment. This will help you release yourself from stress, anxiety, frustration and anger. Instead, focus on positive thoughts and actions that will help you relax and respond in a more balanced way to your everyday life. Letting go of what you can't control, acknowledging your feelings, and taking time for yourself will help you maintain a more positive outlook on life.
With a Zen mindset, you can allow your thoughts, feelings, and perceptions to be what they are without judgment. Circumstances and situations are just occurrences that mean nothing until we place our own subjective ideas and emotions onto them.When acting from a Zen mindset, you relax the negative thoughts and feelings by listening closely to them. Ultimately, this reduces your anxiety, because instead of being fearful of pain, you accept it as part of life. This then allows you to be more focused, relaxed, and attentive to yourself.

This practice isn't about trying to get rid of anything; it's about accepting whatever is there.

Your mind will answer most questions if you learn to relax and wait for the answer.
